01

Activation and growth

Reduced early-stage abandonment by 24%

Direct measured result

The problem

Users were leaving before reaching meaningful value. The team needed to know where they stopped, why they stopped, and which intervention belonged at each stage.

What I owned

  1. InstrumentedDefined an eight-stage funnel and 11 acquisition and activation events across Mixpanel and PostHog.
  2. DiagnosedMapped 12 abandonment stages to observed behavior, likely causes, and recovery paths.
  3. PrioritizedCombined product behavior and user feedback to sequence onboarding and recovery interventions.
  4. MeasuredDefined 22 lifecycle KPIs to monitor progress and warning signals.
